# Women & Girls Empowerment Portal

A digital platform designed to empower women and girls in Nepal by providing access to skill development, health awareness resources, community support, and economic opportunities.

## Features
- Online vocational skill training
- Health and safety awareness resources
- Community mentoring and support
- Access to job and economic opportunities

## Goal
To promote gender equality, independence, and education for women and girls in Nepal through technology.

## Tech Stack
- Next.js
- Node.js
- Database (MongoDB / PostgreSQL)
- Tailwind CSS
